100% Control of your application data within your own environments

Form.io is an enterprise class combined form and API data management platform for developers who are building their own complex form-based business process applications. Our platform is used by companies all over the world, from startups, to SMBs, to large enterprises, who operate in a wide range of business verticals including healthcare, government, banking and finance, insurance, legal, online retail, compliance, multi-tenanted technology platforms, software development agencies, and many more.
Read More

Due to our unique “serverless” application architecture, our forms and APIs can be embedded seamlessly into both the front end and server side of your platform applications, and can solution even the most complex requirements that come with mobilized business process applications. Fully mobilized multi-page forms with conditional logic workflows and offline mode operation, complex user authentication roles and permissions, non-developer simplified form builders embedded directly into your app, and 100% data security and control enabled by on-premise deployments are just the beginning of the many unique capabilities and features you’ll find in Form.io
